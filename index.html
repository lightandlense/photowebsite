Step Inside

Russell Klimas

I discovered light painting photography a few years ago and it quickly became my primary focus. By day I work as a web developer, but after sunset the real work begins — long exposures, strategic planning, and no photo manipulation software. What you see in my images is what happened in front of the camera.

In late 2017, I was diagnosed with testicular cancer. During treatment, light painting became my source of hope and inspiration. After achieving remission — with the support of incredible medical professionals and loved ones — I came out the other side more committed than ever to pursuing this craft full-time.
